Zeno

Zeno is a versatile and user-friendly virtual assistant designed to simplify daily tasks and enhance productivity. It is created to make technology more accessible and practical for everyone, without relying on advanced AI or machine learning. The primary goal of Zeno is to streamline routine tasks such as setting reminders, managing schedules, controlling smart devices, and automating repetitive processes. Through simple voice commands and an intuitive interface, users can manage their day efficiently and seamlessly.

BloodSync

BloodSync is a cutting-edge platform designed to streamline the process of blood donation, distribution, and access for hospitals and individuals in need. The goal of BloodSync is to create a seamless, reliable, and efficient system that connects blood donors with recipients, ensuring that life-saving blood supplies are available where and when they are needed the most. This platform aims to bridge the gap between hospitals, blood banks, and donors by offering a centralized hub for blood donation requests, tracking, and distribution.
